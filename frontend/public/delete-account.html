Request account deletion

Royal Poker Match · Use this page to ask us to delete your account and associated personal data.

Google Play and privacy laws require a clear way to request deletion. Submit the form below (or use Settings → Support in the app). We aim to process verified requests within 30 days.

What we delete

  • Your account login (username / password hash / linked Google sign-in)
  • Cloud save / progress synced to your account
  • Scores, challenge history, and support tickets tied to your account
  • Push notification tokens for your devices

What we may keep briefly

  • Limited purchase or fraud-prevention records required by law or payment providers (kept only as long as necessary, then removed or anonymised)
  • Aggregated / anonymised analytics that cannot identify you

Submit a deletion request

Enter the username on the account you want deleted, plus an email we can use to confirm the request.

Already signed in?

Open the game → Settings → Request account deletion, or send a Support message with the subject “Account deletion request”.
